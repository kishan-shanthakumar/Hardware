Extensions: RV64IMFD_Zifencei

5 stage pipeline: Fetch, Decode, Execute, Memory, Writeback

Fetch: Branch Predictor, Misprediction Flushes, interrupt Flushes
Decode: Fence.i flushes(to prevent additonal stalls)
Execute: Misprediction results

Registers
1. Integer Registers = 32, 64 bit
2. Floating Point Registers = 32, 64 bit

EUs
1. Adder/Subtractor
2. Multiplier/Divider
3. Branch/Jump
